Who We Are

Poshak is an agri-tech and soil health company that provides integrated soil and fertilizer solutions to farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses across India.

We specialize in soil testing, organic and inorganic fertilizers, and long-term soil improvement programs. Backed by agricultural science and a mission to restore soil health, we work to improve yield, preserve nature, and uplift rural livelihoods.
